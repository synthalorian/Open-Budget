## Long description (4000 chars for Play, 4000 for Apple)

```
[Open Budget is a clean, offline-first personal budgeting app that respects your data.

No ads. No accounts. No cloud sync you didn't ask for. Your transactions stay on your
device unless YOU export them.

FEATURES
• Account tracking across checking, savings, credit, cash
• Monthly budgets with rollover
• Category-based spending reports
• Multi-currency support
• Import / export (CSV, OFX)
• Dark mode, native look on Android and iOS

WHY "OPEN"
This app is part of the open-* family of tools built for people who want software
that serves them, not their attention. Source on GitHub. No tracking, no analytics,
no telemetry.

[WHAT IT'S NOT]
• Not a bank integration tool. This is manual entry + imports.
• Not multi-user. One person, one device.
• Not a financial advice tool. You manage your money; it just tracks it.

[Free. Open source. Built by one person.]
```
